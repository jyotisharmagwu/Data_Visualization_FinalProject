About

In todayâ€™s world, tourism sector is an integral part of all countries growth. Over the period of time, tourism industry has expanded and becoming an important contributing factor in the economy of both developed and developing countries. On the global scale, it has become revenue source for various nations. This project uses visualizations to help describe the evolution of Global Travel and Tourism for past 23 years. In order to understand how tourism is a fastest growing economic sector, it is important to understand the contributing factors. The objective of this project is to use visualizations to be able to observe how the Global Travel and Tourism has expanded over the period of time and what are its impact. In the present study, it was also analyzed that how the Tourism has contributed in the growth of countries in terms of GDP and employment contribution.





DataSet



Global Travel and Tourism data was collected from The World Bank and World Travel & Tourism Council (WTTC). The data was available to download in CSV format files from the World Bank itself using tourism indicators and TCdata360 platform of World Bank to access WTTC data.
