Tableau Analysis







Impact of Travel and Tourism

Travel and Tourism do impacts countries in positive manner. In many countries it is the major source of income and is the contributing factor in economy and creating job opportunities. The following analysis will reveal which countries are emerging with growth in employment opportunities and contribution to GDP from Tourism.







Employment Contribution

Travel and Tourism contributes to the economy of various developed and developing nations. In various countries tourism contributes a big role in creating job opportunities. Below Google Bubble chart shows the comparison of total percentage of population employed versus population employed from tourism. For example for an year 2018, Top 5 countries with most contribution in employment coming from Tourism are found : Macao SAR China, Iceland, Maldives, New Zealand, Barbados, Malta, and Cyprus are some of the top countries whose major contribution to employment is from Tourism Industry only.









Travel and Tourism total contribution to GDP

The Bubble Chart shows the comparison between total GDP versus travel and tourism total contribution to GDP. For example for an year 2018 countries like Aruba, Maldives, Seychelles, Macao Special Administrative Region, The Bahamas, and Antigua and Barbuda are the top 5 countries having tourism as their major source of contribution to their countries GDP in comparison to other job sectors. The data varies year to year and hence top country vary from year to year.
